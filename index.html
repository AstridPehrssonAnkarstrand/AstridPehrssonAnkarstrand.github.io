Fear of the Light

UI/UX Design, Unreal Engine 5

Best Design Nominee at the Swedish Game Awards 2024! Released on Steam early november.

A short sci-fi survival horror game set on a spaceship where the light is scarier then the dark. Created for Game Project 3 at Futuregames over seven weeks in a ten people team.
