Catfished

Solo developer, Side-project, UI/UX Design, Unity, Unreal Engine 5

I wrote a short novel that I'm quite fond of but felt the story would be more suited for a different, more interactive medium.
The story? A classic unlikely romance featuring two young adult guys who meet through video games & start developing a close bond.
The catch? Both are pretending to be a girl & are desperate to keep it a secret.

Catfished is an ongoing solo passion-project I've been working on since I finished the first story draft in 2019. The idea is to make a visual novel style game that takes place on a desktop. The project can be broken down into three distinct iterations, the earlier two developed in Unity whilst the current one in Unreal Engine 5.
