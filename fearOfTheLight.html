A short sci-fi survival horror game set on a spaceship where the light is scarier than the dark. Nominated for Best Design at the Swedish Game Awards 2024 and released on Steam.

UI Development

As I’ve never worked with diegetic UI previously I took some time to research other games. Alien Isolation was used as the main inspiration but Fallout: New Vegas’ terminals as well.
It was decided early on we’d use a shader to style the widgets and make them look like a screen. I kept the UI in black and white with a pop of color sparingly. Instead using spacing and other tricks to separate different elements of the UI.

Tools



Navigating in the dark is difficult, especially when you're being hunted down by alien moths. To help the player out a bit they have three different tools they can pick up along the way. To increase immersion I wanted to make the tools diegetic.



Radar


The radar helps the player detect objects of interests, and objects to avoid. It drains battery whilst being used. I made icons to distinquish between the different signals.

During early playtests I noticed the battery level was often missed as it's located in the bottom left corner. To improve this I added the current battery level to the Radar tool screen.





EMP


The EMP can turn off lamps for a short period of time at the cost of 10% of your battery. For the UI I reused the layout for the radar, as it's using the same screen so I felt it made sense for consistency. I also added a crosshair to help inform the player where they're aiming and when they can shoot. I use color to inform the current status. Green for ready, red to warn that an action isn't possible.



Interactive Data Terminal



The data terminal is an important part of the game, letting the player progress further by unlocking new areas. It also contains lore in the form of files and a map of the level.
